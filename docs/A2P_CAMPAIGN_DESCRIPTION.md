## Campaign Description

RIOS LLC CHRIS RIOS SOLE MBR does business as RIOS LLC. This marketing campaign sends recurring SMS messages from RIOS LLC to U.S. customers and prospective customers who voluntarily opt in through the public web form at https://rios-llc.com/.

Messages promote RIOS LLC retail products and may include:

- New product drops
- Member-only offers
- Limited restock alerts
- Promotional discount codes
- Seasonal promotions
- Product updates

Message frequency varies and does not exceed 10 messages per month. Each message identifies RIOS LLC as the sender. Recipients can reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help at any time.

This campaign does not include order confirmations, shipping notifications, account alerts, support replies, appointment messages, lead generation, affiliate marketing, third-party offers, or brokered leads.

## User Workflow

1. A customer visits the public website at https://rios-llc.com/.
2. The customer enters first name, last name, email address, and U.S. mobile phone number.
3. The form displays a separate optional marketing SMS consent checkbox.
4. The checkbox is not pre-checked.
5. The customer is enrolled in SMS only if they actively check the marketing SMS consent box.
6. The consent disclosure identifies RIOS LLC as the sender, describes the marketing message types, states message frequency, discloses message and data rates, includes HELP and STOP instructions, and states that consent is not a condition of purchase.
7. The form links to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service at the time of opt-in.
8. After submission, customers who selected SMS consent see a confirmation with the same SMS program disclosures.

## Exact Consent Language

I consent to receive recurring marketing text messages from RIOS LLC at the phone number provided about new product drops, member-only offers, limited restocks, promotional discount codes, and product updates. Message frequency varies, up to 10 messages per month. Message & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help and STOP to opt out. Consent is not a condition of purchase.
