A2P SMS Opt-In Proof

This page documents the public opt-in workflow for the RIOS LLC VIP List marketing SMS program.

Campaign Summary

RIOS LLC operates a U.S.-based retail business. Customers may opt in to receive recurring marketing text messages from RIOS LLC about new product drops, member-only offers, limited restock alerts, promotional discount codes, and product updates.

The public sender/program name shown to customers is RIOS LLC. If the A2P registration dashboard displays the tax/legal name as RIOS LLC CHRIS RIOS SOLE MBR, the customer-facing brand and SMS program are still presented as RIOS LLC.

Only Opt-In Method

  • Method: Public website form with an optional marketing SMS checkbox
  • Form URL: https://rios-llc.com/
  • Proof URL: https://rios-llc.com/a2p-opt-in-proof.html
  • Privacy Policy: https://rios-llc.com/legal/privacy-policy.html
  • Terms of Service: https://rios-llc.com/legal/terms-of-service.html

RIOS LLC does not collect SMS opt-ins by paper form, verbal script, keyword, hidden form, login-only form, third-party list, purchased list, affiliate list, or lead broker.

Phone Number Collection

Customers enter their first name, last name, email address, and U.S. mobile phone number directly into the public form. The SMS checkbox is not pre-selected. A customer is enrolled in SMS only if they actively check the marketing SMS consent box before submitting the form.

Exact Consent Language

Required Disclosures Shown At Opt-In

  • Program name and sender: RIOS LLC VIP List marketing SMS program
  • Message content: product drops, member-only offers, restocks, discount codes, and product updates
  • Message frequency: varies, up to 10 messages per month
  • Fee disclosure: Message and data rates may apply
  • Customer care: Reply HELP for help, or contact Hello@rioscontact.me or (213) 663-0834
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to opt out
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase
  • Privacy Policy and Terms of Service links are visible at the time of phone number collection

No Third-Party Sharing

RIOS LLC does not sell, rent, trade, share, or transfer SMS opt-in data or consent records to third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in data is used only to operate the RIOS LLC messaging program and to maintain compliance records.

Opt-Out And Help

  • HELP response: RIOS LLC: For help, email Hello@rioscontact.me or call (213) 663-0834. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • STOP response: RIOS LLC: You have successfully unsubscribed and will no longer receive text messages from this number.
